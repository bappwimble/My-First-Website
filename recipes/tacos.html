Tacos

ingredients

  • one pound of ground beef
  • a stick of cheese
  • a head of lettuce
  • two whole tomatos
  • taco meat seasoning
  • hard or soft shells
  • sour cream
  • salsa

steps

  1. preheat stovetop to medium heat
  2. put ground beef into a pot or pan and break up into small chunks
  3. make sure to mix and flip often so it cooks evenly
  4. while the meat cooks grate your cheese, cube your tomatos, cut your lettuce into small strips and prepare plates and ingredient bowls for serving
  5. once the meat is fully browned add in your taco seasoning to your liking and mix in
  6. when the meat is fully seasoned and cooked put it in the pot on a hot pad alongside the rest of the ingredients
  7. add the meat, cheese,lettuce, tomato and or salsa, and sour cream to your desired shell
  8. enjoy!
