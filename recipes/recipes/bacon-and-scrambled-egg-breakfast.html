Bacon & Eggs

ingredients

  • pack of salted bacon
  • two large eggs
  • salt, pepper, and other spices to your liking
  • rubber mixing spatula
  • normal metal spatula

steps

  1. preheat two heat sources on your stovetop to medium
  2. crack eggs into a bowl or cup and mix with a fork until yolk and whites combine
  3. grab some strips of bacon and cut them in half for easier cooking
  4. put butter in the egg pan
  5. pour the egg mix into the egg pan and place the bacon into the bacon pan
  6. let the bacon cook for about five minutes before flipping
  7. while the bacon cooks make sure to frequently flip and stir your eggs so they cook evenly
  8. after the eggs start solidifying you can season them and it will not change the texture
  9. once you flip the bacon get a plate with some paper towel to put the bacon on to dry and let it cook for five more minutes
  10. by the time the bacon is done and on the plate the eggs should be done as well and you can plate and serve it
  11. enjoy!
