KRAFT Mac & Cheese

ingredients

  • one box of kraft mac and cheese
  • powdered cheese packet
  • one quarter cup of milk
  • one tablespoon of butter

steps

  1. preheat stovetop to high for boiling
  2. fill a pot quarter way with water
  3. pour in macaroni and turn heat down slightly once the water comes to a rolling boil
  4. in the mean time setup a strainer over your sink to pour the cooked noodles in
  5. stir often making sure the noodles don't stick to the bottom
  6. once noodles are cooked to your liking, pour into strainer and let sit
  7. move pot to another heat source and turn to low
  8. drop in butter and let it melt
  9. once butter is melted pour in the noodles and milk, stir
  10. after the noodles are mixed in pour in the powdered cheese mix and keep stiring until all noodles are coated
  11. serve and enjoy!
